Book reviews

I've always loved reading, and in 2017 I opened an Instagram account (if you want to see it it's @rana_lettrice, the reviews are in Italian, but you might enjoy my photos .^.) where I used to recommend books. But I don't like Instagram anymore so I deleted Instagram from my phone, I learnt how to create websites and I started this blog.
I am from Italy and I read mostly Italian books, but sometimes (in fact, everytime I can find a book in English in the library or online by an author I think I can understand well enough) I read in English too. I wouldn't post here in the English section Italian books because of the quotes: I wouldn't translate them right and I don't want to disrespect the author by quoting him wrong.
By the way, my recommendations will contain:

  • the plot of the book, of course
  • my opinion on the book
  • some nice quotes
  • a part where I will say my thoughts in some scenes (this part will contain spoilers)
